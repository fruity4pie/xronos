XRONOS

XRONOS is an aggregator of cryptocurrency and blockchain-technologies.

Target

XRONOS target is to create the first truly stable cryptocurrency.

Mission

XRONOS mission is to create truly decentralized, stable and massively-applied payment instrument, which will accelerate the process of the fiat money transformation into cryptocurrency, thus helping to proceed from outdated banking system to the new phase of the world economy evolution.

Advantages of the system

  • Evolutionary concept, unique stack of reliable technologies

  • Self-optimizing blockchain

  • Joint use of eight symmetric and asymmetric hashing algorithms

  • XRSS - system of rate stabilization based on adaptation of collective decisions by ring signature and zero-knowledge-proof technologies

  • Unique combination of PoW and PoS technologies and CryptoNight and Zero-knowledge-proof algorithms

  • Simplicity of use and integration

  • Network elasticity, expandability and scalability

  • The technology enabling reformation of labor markets and world legal mechanism
